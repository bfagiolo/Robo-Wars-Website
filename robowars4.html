Meet The Team Members






"People ask me why I can handle pressure very well. The truth is...this wasnt always the case. It was a trial and error process. I can thank the team at RoboWars Robotics club for that."

- Club Alumni, Robert Downey Jr.





"The reason it has taken so long for the robotics industry to move forward is because people keep trying to make something that is cool but difficult to achieve rather than trying to find solutions to actual human problems. Technology can be extremely expensive if you don't focus."

- 1st year member, Albert Einstein





"When brainstorming the design of my machine, I try not to treat it as some sort of strict process like the scientific method. I prefer to treat problem-solving as more of an art than anything else."

- Team Captain, Andrew Garfield
