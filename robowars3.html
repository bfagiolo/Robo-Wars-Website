Frequently Asked Questions

Q: When are the club meetings?
A: We meet 3 times a week at 7:30pm in the Fullerton Engineering School Building, room 305.
Q: Do I need prior knowledge of robotics to join?
A: While prior knowledge is preferred and even encouraged, it is not a requirement to join.
Q: What if I do not want to be apart of the competitions?
A: The competitions are the best part. Yet, you can always take a backseat role if you prefer to not participate in the challenges.
Q: Is this club expensive?
A: No, this club is actually entirely free. You only have to pay for travel expenses on your own
Q: Are the fights intense?
A: If a particular exhibition happens to be a fight, then it depends on the specific rules for that competition.
